**Starter rules – please review and edit to match your personal style and the specific job you are targeting.**

### Length & Brevity
- Keep the resume to **one page** (ideal for < 10 years experience).  
- Use concise bullet points; each bullet ≤ 2 lines.

### Section Order (recommended)
1. **Header** – name, title, contact (email, phone, LinkedIn, GitHub).  
2. **Professional Summary** – 2‑3 sentence pitch (tailor to each role).  
3. **Key Skills / Technologies** – top 6‑8 items, grouped by category (Programming, Cloud, Mobile, Leadership).  
4. **Professional Experience** – reverse‑chronological, with **company, role, location, dates**, followed by 3‑4 achievement bullets per role.  
5. **Education** – degree, institution, dates, relevant coursework or projects (if space permits).  
6. **Additional Sections** (optional): Certifications, Open‑source contributions, Awards.

### Tone & Language
- Use **action‑oriented verbs** (designed, implemented, led, optimized).  
- Quantify impact where possible (e.g., “improved API latency by 15%”) **only if the data exists**; otherwise keep statements factual.  
- Maintain a **professional, confident** tone without exaggerated claims.

### ATS‑Friendliness
- Avoid graphics, tables, or complex columns.  
- Use standard headings (“Professional Experience”, “Education”).  
- Include **keywords** from the job description (e.g., “Java”, “AWS”, “Android”, “microservices”).  
- Save the final file as **PDF** (text‑selectable) and also keep a **plain‑text version** for copy‑paste into applicant portals.

### Styling & Formatting
- Font: **Sans‑serif** (e.g., Calibri, Arial) size 10–11 pt for body, 14–16 pt for name.  
- Margins: 0.5–0.75 in all sides.  
- Consistent date format (Month Year).  
- Bullet style: simple solid circles or hyphens.  
- Ensure **consistent tense**: present tense for current role, past tense for previous roles.

### Customization Tips
- **Target Industries:** Emphasize E‑commerce, Cloud, Mobile, and Web projects when applying to related roles.  
- **Leadership:** Highlight any team‑lead or mentorship experiences, even informal, under the “Leadership” skill.  
- **Projects/Certifications:** If you have AWS certifications or personal Android apps, add a short “Certifications & Projects” section.

--- 

*Adjust these guidelines as needed to reflect your personal achievements and the specific requirements of each application.*
